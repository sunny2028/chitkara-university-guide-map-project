THE BLOCKS

Babbage Block



This is main block of Chitkara University, and it is named after because it rhymes Charles Babbage who was an English polymath. A mathematician, philosopher, inventor and mechanical engineer, Babbage originated the concept of a digital programmable computer. This block has 5 floors, which includes Acoount department, Examination cell,Curin ,Foreign exchange department.

Turing block



This block is named after the great Mathematician,Computer Scientist Alan Mathison Turing. This block is mainly for 1st year student who are under Chitkara college of Applied Engineering. It has 5 floors. It also has Chemistry, Physics, BEE ,Computer labs.

Newton Block



This block is named after a great mathematician, astronomer, theologian, author and physicist , Issac Newton . This block is for Computer science Studnets only, and has 2 floors. It only has computer labs and a few classrooms . It has a special lab which has only apple desktop computers.

Edison Block



This block is named after an American inventor and businessman, Thomas Edison. This block is for Electronics students , and has 3 floors . There are digital electronic labs in the ground floor.

Escoffier Block



This block is named after Georges Auguste Escoffier who was a French chef, restaurateur and culinary writer who popularized and updated traditional French cooking methods. Le-corbusier Block



This block is named after Charles-Édouard Jeanneret, known as Le Corbusier who was a Swiss-French architect, designer, painter, urban planner, writer, and one of the pioneers of what is now called modern architecture. This block is for Architecture department . It has 2 floors and very big class rooms.

Fleming Block



This block is named after Sir Alexander Fleming was a Scottish physician, microbiologist, and pharmacologist.This block has 4 floors in which one is a hall.This block is for BBA ,MBA and Pharmcy department. It has a very beautiful statue of Saraswati MATA in front of it and a fountain also.

Galileo Block



This block is named after Galileo Galilei who was a central figure in the transition from natural philosophy to modern science and in the transformation of the scientific Renaissance into a scientific revolution. This block is for Nursing and physiology department and has 5 floors.

Demorgan Block



This block is named after Augustus De Morgan was a British mathematician and logician. This block is the smallest block in Chitkara University. This is for BCA department and has 1 floor only.
